The Beatles

My Favorite Music Group

The Beatles are my favorite music group because they changed music forever with their creativity, songwriting, and unique sound. The group was made up of John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison, and Ringo Starr. Their music ranges from classic rock songs to emotional ballads, and they influenced countless artists around the world.

My favorite Beatles song is "Blackbird" because Paul McCartney wrote it as a message inspired by the civil rights movement and the struggles Black Americans faced during the 1960s. I like the meaning behind the song and its peaceful acoustic sound.
